The Gap

What's happening today and what changes

The business runs well. The problem is that too much of the early work — collecting customer details, recommending a scheme, chasing documents — is done manually by agents on calls. The app takes that over.

Today
One agent, one customer, one call at a time

NeoDove dials the customer. Agent collects business details, recommends a scheme, and sells via QR — all manually on the phone.

Zoho Forms and Zoho Projects are separate

After a customer converts, the agent fills a Zoho form. That goes to Zoho Projects. The ops manager then manually assigns it to a loan supervisor. No single view of any of it.

Documents come in over WhatsApp

Loan supervisor collects Aadhaar, PAN, caste certificate, bank passbook etc. over WhatsApp. Everything stored as files in Zoho Projects. No structure.

Customer has no idea where their application is

No tracking, no updates. They have to call or message to find out what's happening.

After this build
App collects the first half via Voice AI

Customer opens the app. The AI asks about their business in Hindi or Kannada — voice or text. Agent only needs to step in after the customer has paid ₹1,999.

One portal for the whole ops team

We're building the CRM in-house. Admin assigns cases to loan supervisors from the same platform. No switching between tools.

Documents uploaded in the app

Customer uploads directly from their phone. Each document is checked on upload and flagged if something is wrong before the team sees it.

Customer tracks their application themselves

Every stage update shows in the app. They can chat with their loan supervisor, raise a ticket, and see exactly where things stand without calling anyone.

Scope

What we're building

Four things. Nothing else is in scope for this engagement.

New

Customer Mobile App

iOS and Android. Voice AI onboarding in Hindi and Kannada, scheme recommendation, document upload, ₹1,999 token payment, live application tracker, in-app chat with loan supervisor, scheme browsing, content pages, and support tickets.

New

Admin Portal

Web-based internal tool. Two roles — Administrator and Manager. Full pipeline view across all stages. Document review, stage management, application assignment, and activity log with NeoDove call history per case.

New

In-House CRM

Built into the admin portal. No Zoho CRM or Zoho Projects dependency. All application data, documents, stage history, and call logs live in one place owned by LoanDoctor.

Connecting existing

NeoDove

NeoDove stays as the calling tool. Every call a sales agent or loan supervisor makes is automatically attached to that customer's case. No manual logging needed.

What we understood from the client — and how we're building it

This is for the dev team. Flows only — no UI specs. Read this completely before starting. The three sections below cover how the client works today, how the customer app works, and how the admin portal works.

NeoDove

Collects business info on call

Business type, turnover, location, category, extra info

PMEGP, MSME, Mudra, NLM, Stand-Up India, or Education Loan

Sells via QR code

Customer not converted

They leave. Lead stays in NeoDove. No automated follow-up currently in place.

The problem: This entire journey runs across NeoDove, WhatsApp, Zoho Forms, and Zoho Projects — four separate places. No single view. The platform we're building replaces this chain.

Admin portal — ops team flow

Administrator

Portal owner and admin. Full access. Maps to "Portal Owner" and "Admin" roles in the current Zoho setup.
  • See all applications across all stages
  • Assign applications to loan supervisors
  • Add or remove users, manage roles
  • View all reports, revenue, agent commissions
  • Move applications between stages
  • Upload documents from the admin side

Manager

Loan supervisors, sales agents. Sees only what's assigned to them. Maps to "Manager" role in the current Zoho setup.
  • View and work on their assigned cases only
  • Move applications through stages they own
  • Chat with the customer in-app
  • Review, approve, or reject documents
  • Upload documents from the admin side
  • See NeoDove call history for their cases
Trigger
Payment confirmed

Case appears in the portal at "New Application." Administrator gets notified.

Admin assigns to a loan supervisor

Administrator picks a Manager from the team and assigns the case to them

Loan supervisor opens the case

They see the customer profile, AI-collected data, uploaded documents, and full NeoDove call history

Chat with customer

Loan supervisor messages the customer directly in-app. Replaces WhatsApp for ops communication on this case.

Get or upload documents

Request a document from the customer (they upload in the app) or upload directly from the admin side. Both tracked against the case.

Move through pipeline stages

Supervisor moves the case forward. Each stage change sends a push notification to the customer.

Project delivered

Project report prepared, loan submitted to bank, documents sent to customer. Case marked complete.

NeoDove — how calls attach to cases
NeoDove
Agent makes a call

Sales agent or loan supervisor calls the customer via NeoDove — before or after payment

Webhook
Call ends → NeoDove fires a webhook

Sends: agent name, customer phone, call duration, notes if entered, timestamp

Call is matched to the case

System matches the phone number to the application. Call log is written to that case's activity timeline.

Loan supervisor sees full call history

Every call ever made to this customer appears in the case — agent name, duration, and notes — before they even pick up the phone

Sales pipeline: NeoDove also handles the sales side — agent commissions and payment tracking (unpaid / partially paid / fully paid). This view is inside the admin portal for Administrator access only.
