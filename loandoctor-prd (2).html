The Problem

What's happening today and what changes

The business runs well. The issue is that every customer needs a sales agent on the phone before anything can happen. The app handles that first conversation — and feeds warm, paying leads directly to the team.

Today
One agent, one customer, one call at a time

NeoDove dials the customer. Agent collects business details, recommends a scheme, and sells via QR code — all manually on the phone. No call can happen in parallel without more agents.

Zoho Forms and Zoho Projects are not connected

Converted customer → agent fills Zoho form → response lands in Zoho Projects → ops manager manually assigns to a loan supervisor. Four steps, three tools, all manual.

Documents come in over WhatsApp

Loan supervisor chases Aadhaar, PAN, caste certificate, bank passbook over WhatsApp. Stored as raw files in Zoho Projects. No structure, no tracking.

Customer has no visibility into their application

No tracking, no updates. They call or WhatsApp to find out what is happening. Every status check is manual work for the team.

After this build
App handles the first conversation via Voice AI

Customer opens the app, the AI asks the right questions in Hindi or Kannada. If they drop off or don't engage, the lead is automatically sent to a sales agent for a follow-up call.

Payment triggers everything automatically

Customer pays ₹1,999 in the app → case is created in the CRM → assigned to a loan supervisor. No form, no manual step, no delay.

One portal for the whole team

CRM built in-house. Administrator assigns, Managers work their cases. No switching between Zoho tools. NeoDove call logs attach to the case automatically.

Customer tracks their application themselves

Every stage update shows in the app with a push notification. They chat with their loan supervisor in-app and upload documents without calling anyone.

Five systems. Nothing else is in scope for this engagement.

Admin Dashboard

Connecting existing

NeoDove Integration

NeoDove stays as the calling tool for sales agents and loan supervisors. We connect it so every call automatically attaches to the right case in the portal — no manual logging. NeoDove also receives leads that dropped off in the app so agents can follow up. Commission tracking from NeoDove's existing data will feed into the sales dashboard.

Block 3
Sales agent — the loop that never disappears
Important: The AI handles the first touch when a customer comes in through the app. But not every customer will go through the AI or pay immediately. The sales agent is still fully in the loop — they get leads from the app, follow up via NeoDove, and can close the sale manually.
Where sales agents get leads
From the app — AI drop-offs

Customer opened the app, AI collected some or all profile data, but they didn't pay. This lead arrives in the agent's queue with everything the AI collected.

From the app — didn't engage with AI

Customer installed and opened the app but didn't speak to the AI at all. Arrives as a basic lead with just name and phone number. Agent calls to qualify.

Direct / walk-in leads

Leads that come in through any other channel — WhatsApp inquiries, website, referrals. Agent creates the lead manually in the sales portal.

What the sales agent can do with each lead
NeoDove
Calls the lead

Via NeoDove dialer. Call auto-attaches to the lead record after the call ends.

Adds notes to the lead

What was discussed, objections, follow-up date. All stored against the lead in the sales portal.

Sends payment QR / link

Agent sends the customer a direct link to pay ₹1,999. Same payment flow as the in-app payment.

Automated
Customer pays → case created

Same outcome as the app flow. Case lands in the admin portal, agent gets commission credit.

Sales agent dashboard — what they see
Lead pool

All leads assigned to them. Filtered by status — new / contacted / follow-up / closed / lost.

Lead detail

Customer profile, AI-collected data, document upload status, full call history from NeoDove, all notes added by this agent.

Commission tracker

Current month earnings, pending approval, historical payouts. Broken down per scheme and per order.

Performance numbers

How many leads contacted, how many converted, conversion rate, monthly target vs actual. Visible to the agent and to the Administrator.

Commission flow: When a customer pays ₹1,999 and the agent is credited — commission is calculated automatically (percentage or fixed, set per scheme by admin). It goes into "pending" state. Finance team reviews and approves from the admin panel. After approval, it shows as "confirmed" in the agent's dashboard.

Ops flow — from payment to delivery
Auto trigger
Payment confirmed

Case appears in the portal at "New Application." Administrator sees it immediately.

Admin assigns to loan supervisor

Administrator picks a Manager and assigns. That Manager sees the case in their queue.

Supervisor opens the case

Sees full customer profile, AI-collected data, uploaded docs, NeoDove call history, and activity timeline

Works the checklist

Each stage has a configurable checklist of steps. Supervisor ticks them off as they complete each one. Checklist is set per scheme by the Administrator.

Chats with customer in-app

Replaces WhatsApp for case communication. Full conversation visible in the case timeline.

Requests or reviews documents

Can request a specific document (customer uploads in app) or upload directly from admin side. Approve or reject each doc with a note.

Moves case to next stage

When all checklist steps are done, supervisor moves to the next stage. Customer gets a push notification. Activity log records the move with timestamp and agent name.

Block 5
Ops pipeline — six stages
Every paid application moves through these stages in order
01
New Application
Entry: payment confirmed. Admin assigns to supervisor.
02
Eligibility Check
Screening team. Verify scheme match manually.
03
Documents Pending
Documentation team. Review, approve or reject each doc.
04
Project Report
Project report team. DPR prepared and uploaded.
05
Delivery Ready
Delivery team. Submit to bank portal, send docs to customer.
—
On Hold
Any stage, any team. Bank query, missing doc, unresponsive customer.
On Hold: Any case can be moved to Hold from any stage by either role. When the issue is resolved, the case goes back to the stage it came from — not to the beginning. Customer sees the reason for the hold in their app.

Checklist per stage: Each stage has a configurable checklist. The Administrator sets the steps for each scheme (e.g. PMEGP has different steps to Mudra). Supervisors work through the checklist and the system tracks which steps are done.
Block 6
NeoDove — how it connects to the platform
NeoDove
Agent makes a call

Sales agent or loan supervisor dials the customer via NeoDove — before or after payment

Webhook
Call ends → NeoDove fires a webhook

Payload: agent name, customer phone, duration, notes if added, timestamp

Call matched to the case or lead

System matches phone number to an application or lead record. Call is written to that record's activity timeline.

Supervisor or agent sees full history

Every call ever made to this person shows in their record — agent name, duration, notes — before the next call is made

Two places NeoDove connects:
1. Ops pipeline — call logs attach to the customer's case. Loan supervisor sees full call history per case.
2. Sales portal — call logs attach to the lead. Sales agent sees call history per lead. Commission data from NeoDove feeds into the commission dashboard.
