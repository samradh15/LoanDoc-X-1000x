Where Things Stand

The honest problem with how things run today

LoanDoctor works. The numbers prove it. But the current setup is built around people doing repetitive work that software should be doing. That's the gap we're here to close.

Current State — Bottlenecks
Everything depends on sales agents Onboarding a single client takes manual work — calls, form filling, follow-ups. One agent can only handle so many clients. Growth hits a ceiling.
There is no pipeline visibility at all Forms get filled and submitted, but no one can see where each applicant stands, what stage they are at, or which ones need a follow-up. It all lives in someone's head or a spreadsheet.
NeoDove calls exist in isolation Call data from NeoDove and client data are two completely separate places. When an advisor calls someone, they have no history in front of them — every call starts from scratch.
No mobile app for the client Applicants have no self-serve way to track their loan status, upload documents, or get answers without calling or messaging someone.
Target State — What We're Building
Voice AI handles first contact and onboarding A voice assistant asks the right questions, maps the applicant to the right scheme, and guides them through document upload — no human needed for this part.
A built-in CRM gives full pipeline visibility Every lead, document, call, and stage is tracked inside the platform we build — no third-party tool, no monthly licensing. The team sees a live dashboard of every active application.
NeoDove call logs connect directly to each client record When a call is logged in NeoDove, it automatically attaches to that client's profile in the platform. Before any advisor calls someone, they see the full history right there.
Client-facing mobile app with real-time tracking Applicants open the app, talk to the AI assistant, upload documents, and track their loan progress at every stage — like tracking a Swiggy order, but for loans.
How It Works

The full journey, step by step

From the moment a person opens the app to the day their loan is disbursed — here is every step, who handles it, and where the system does the heavy lifting automatically.

Phase A — Pre-Application
Voice AI
01
Lead Capture via Voice AI
User opens app. The AI assistant asks simple questions — what kind of business, income, location, category. Answers go straight into the in-house CRM as a new lead record.
Automated
02
Scheme Eligibility Test
System matches answers against PMEGP, MSME, Mudra, NLM, Stand-Up rules. Shows applicant which scheme fits and why.
AI-Guided

Document Scrutiny
AI tells user exactly which documents are needed for their scheme. User uploads via app. System checks format, clarity, and completeness.

Feasibility Check
Project cost, margin money, and loan amount are validated. Loan officer is flagged if anything needs manual review. Most cases pass automatically.
Payment Gate — Before Application Proceeds
Token Payment of ₹1,999
Once eligibility is confirmed and documents are verified, the applicant pays a one-time processing fee of ₹1,999 via UPI, card, or net banking. Only after this does the application get formally submitted and assigned to the LoanDoctor team.
Phase B — Digital Application
05
Online Application Form
Pre-filled from the onboarding conversation. User confirms and signs. Scheme-specific fields are shown dynamically based on loan type.
06
Loan and Subsidy Details
System calculates exact loan amount, applicable subsidy percentage, margin money required, and recommended bank based on location and scheme.
Automated
07
Error-Free Validation
Every field and document is cross-checked before submission. Missing or mismatched data is flagged to the user with a clear fix instruction. Nothing gets submitted incomplete.
Automated
08
Submit to Bank and Govt Portal
Application is submitted to the relevant bank portal and government scheme portal simultaneously. Confirmation references are saved in the in-house CRM automatically — nothing gets lost.
Phase C — Post-Application
09
Objection Handling
When a bank raises a query, the CRM flags it and assigns it to the loan advisor. NeoDove triggers an automated call task. Client is notified through the app.
10

Tracked

Once sanctioned, the CRM record is updated. Client receives in-app notification. Subsidy details are locked and confirmed in the system for audit trail.
AI Follow-up
12
Disbursement and Monitoring
Post-disbursement, AI voice assistant follows up at 30/60/90-day intervals to check utilisation. Responses are logged in CRM for government reporting.
The Core Differentiator

What Voice AI actually does inside the app

This is not a chatbot with pre-set menus. The voice assistant holds a real conversation, understands context in Hindi and Kannada, and hands off to a human only when it genuinely needs to.

Touchpoint 01 — First Open
Discovery conversation
The AI introduces itself and asks the user what they're trying to do. Not a form — an actual exchange. It adapts follow-up questions based on each answer.
"Namaste, main aapko sahi scheme dhundne mein help karunga. Aap kya kaam karte hain — koi business hai ya naya shuru karna chahte hain?"
Touchpoint 02 — Scheme Selection
Plain-language recommendation
After gathering details, the AI explains which scheme fits and why — in simple terms. It tells the user what subsidy they qualify for, what the loan limit is, and what they need to bring.
"Aapke liye PMEGP best hai. Aapko 25% subsidy milegi aur loan 10 lakh tak ho sakta hai. Iske liye sirf Aadhaar, PAN, aur ek project report chahiye."
Touchpoint 03 — Document Upload
Guided document collection
The AI walks the user through uploading each document one at a time. It checks the image quality and tells them if something is blurry or cut off before moving on.
"Abhi Aadhaar card upload karein — front aur back dono. Ensure karein ki sab kuch clearly visible ho, corners cut na hon."

Component Status What it does in the new system

Voice AI Assistant New Build Handles lead capture, scheme selection guidance, and document collection conversations in Hindi and Kannada. Replaces the sales agent for first-touch onboarding.
In-House CRM Dashboard New Build Built inside the platform — not a third-party tool. Loan advisors see every lead, their current stage, document status, and call history in one place. No Zoho dependency, no licensing cost, fully tailored to how LoanDoctor actually works.
In-House Application Forms New Build Dynamic forms built into the app and the advisor dashboard. Each scheme (PMEGP, MSME, Mudra, etc.) has its own field set. Data from the Voice AI onboarding pre-fills everything — the applicant rarely types from scratch.
Token Payment Gateway (₹1,999) New Build After eligibility and document checks pass, the applicant pays ₹1,999 via UPI, card, or net banking before their application is formally forwarded. Payment confirmation auto-updates the application stage in the in-house CRM and triggers the team assignment.
NeoDove Existing + Enhanced Call logs from NeoDove now automatically sync to the matching client record inside the in-house CRM via webhook. Advisors see the full call history before they dial. No manual note-taking, no switching tabs.
Document Validation Engine New Build Checks uploaded documents for image quality, document type correctness, and field completeness before the application moves forward.
Scheme Eligibility Engine New Build A smart matching system built into the platform. It takes the applicant's details — age, category, income, location, type of business — and tells them exactly which government scheme fits them best. LoanDoctor's ops team can update the rules as schemes change.
Loan Status Tracker New Build Live view inside the app showing exactly where the application is at any given moment — submitted, under review, sanctioned, disbursed. Pulls directly from the in-house CRM pipeline stages.
Bank and Govt Portal Submission Existing + Automated Right now an agent does this manually for every application. We will automate it directly through the bank's portal API wherever they allow it. Where they don't, the advisor gets a structured task in the platform to complete it in one step instead of navigating portals from scratch each time.
Post-Disbursement AI Follow-up New Build Voice AI calls the applicant at 30, 60, and 90 days after disbursement to check how the loan is being used. Responses are logged in the in-house CRM automatically — useful for government compliance reporting.
